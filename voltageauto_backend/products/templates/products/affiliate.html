Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the affiliate program?

Simply click the "Join Now" button below and fill out the form with your details. We'll verify and approve your application within 24 hours.

Payouts are processed within 48 hours after a confirmed sale. Elite members get instant payouts. You can choose your preferred payment method: bank transfer, mobile money, or wallet.

Is there a limit to how much I can earn?

No! There's no limit on earnings. The more you refer, the more you earn. Top affiliates can earn thousands monthly.

Absolutely! You can promote through Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, TikTok, and any other platform. We encourage creative marketing strategies.

We offer a 30-day cookie window. If your referral returns and purchases within 30 days, you'll still earn the commission.
